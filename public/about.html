Our Mission

Lalabuba was built on one simple idea: every child deserves access to creative tools.

Drawing and coloring nurture imagination, improve fine motor skills, and teach patience. But art supplies cost money, and art classes cost more. Lalabuba uses AI to give every kid an unlimited, personalized coloring book — for free.

Type anything you can imagine. Get a unique coloring page in seconds. Color it in, print it, or race a friend to finish first. No account, no fees, no ads. Just creativity.

Built for Families

🔒

Safe for Kids

Built-in content safety filters block inappropriate subjects in all 12 languages. No ads, no social features, no data collection.

🌍

Truly Multilingual

Full interface translations in English, German, French, Spanish, Russian, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Polish, Turkish, Chinese, and Hindi.

📱

Every Platform

Works in any browser on desktop, tablet, or phone. Native iOS and Android apps available for the best touch experience.

🎯

Age-Appropriate

Four difficulty levels from Easy (toddler-friendly, 6 colors) to Extreme (adult-level mandala complexity). There's a perfect setting for every age.

🖨️

Print-Friendly

Every coloring page can be printed for offline coloring. Great for school projects, birthday parties, and rainy day activities.

🏆

Makes Coloring Social

The Challenge mode and Daily Challenge turn solitary coloring into a shared family or classroom activity.

Part of the Bonifatus Family

Lalabuba is one of several apps developed under the Bonifatus umbrella — a small independent studio based in Germany, dedicated to building useful, beautiful, and free tools for families.

We believe technology should enrich childhood, not exploit it. That's why all our apps are free, contain no ads, and collect the minimum data possible.

Questions or feedback? We'd love to hear from you at info@lalabuba.com or via our contact form.
