Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Lalabuba ("we", "our", "the app") is a free AI coloring page generator for kids and families. This policy explains what information is collected when you use Lalabuba at lalabuba.com or via our iOS or Android app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Lalabuba does not require you to create an account. We do not collect, store, or share:

  • Names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • The coloring pages you create or save
  • Your location
  • Payment information (the app is free)

2. Information That Is Collected

Usage analytics (Google Analytics & PostHog): On our website we use two analytics tools — Google Analytics and PostHog (hosted in the EU) — to understand how the app is used in aggregate, e.g. which features are popular and what languages users prefer. Both are loaded only after you accept analytics cookies via our consent banner, and neither runs if you decline. They collect anonymised data such as browser type, device type, and pages visited; we do not send personally identifiable information and PostHog autocapture is disabled. You can withdraw consent at any time by clicking "Cookie settings" in the footer, or opt out of Google Analytics via the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Mobile app device identifier: The iOS and Android apps generate a random identifier (a UUID) the first time they launch and store it only on your device. It is sent with each drawing request purely to prevent abuse and enforce daily limits. It is not an advertising ID, is not linked to your identity, contains no personal information, and is removed when you uninstall the app. The mobile apps do not load Google Analytics or PostHog.

Bot protection (Cloudflare Turnstile): When you click "Draw!", a Cloudflare Turnstile challenge verifies the request comes from a human. Turnstile processes your request without setting tracking cookies. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy for details.

AI image generation: We use a cascade of AI image generation services including Pollinations.ai, Together AI, Cloudflare Workers AI, and Novita.ai. The subject text you type is forwarded to generate your coloring page. It is not stored beyond the duration of the request.

Shared coloring pages: If you use the "Challenge a friend" feature, your coloring page image is temporarily stored on our server (Hetzner, Germany) for up to 7 days to generate a shareable link. It is automatically deleted after 7 days.

3. Children's Privacy

Lalabuba is designed to be safe for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app contains no advertising, no in-app purchases, and no social networking features. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics — anonymised usage statistics (web, opt-in)
  • PostHog — anonymised product analytics, EU-hosted (web, opt-in)
  • Cloudflare Turnstile — bot protection
  • Pollinations.ai — AI image generation
  • Together AI — AI image generation
  • Cloudflare Workers AI — AI image generation
  • Novita.ai — AI image generation
  • Hetzner Online GmbH — web hosting and image storage (Germany, EU)

5. Cookies & Local Storage

Essential (no consent required):

  • Cloudflare Turnstile — sets a short-lived session token to verify that requests come from humans. No tracking cookies are set. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • localStorage (gallery & preferences) — Lalabuba stores your saved coloring pages and app preferences (language, options) locally in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us.

Analytics (opt-in only):

  • Google Analytics — only loaded after you explicitly accept analytics cookies via our consent banner. Collects anonymised data such as browser type, device type, and pages visited. No personally identifiable information is sent. You can opt out at any time via the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.
  • PostHog (EU-hosted) — only loaded after you explicitly accept analytics cookies. Records anonymised pageviews to understand feature usage; autocapture is disabled and no personally identifiable information is sent. Declining the banner prevents it from loading entirely.

You can withdraw analytics consent at any time by clicking "Cookie settings" in the footer.

6. Data Retention

Shared coloring page images are deleted automatically after 7 days. Analytics data is retained by Google for 26 months per their standard policy. No other data is stored by us.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is generally nothing to access, correct, or delete. Where analytics are involved, you control them entirely through the cookie consent banner.

EU/EEA & UK (GDPR): You have the right to access, rectify, erase, or restrict processing of your data, to data portability, and to object to processing. As analytics run only with your consent, you can exercise these rights at any time by declining or withdrawing consent via "Cookie settings". The legal basis for analytics is your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).

California (CCPA/CPRA): We do not sell or share personal information, and we do not collect the categories of personal information that would trigger a sale. California residents may still contact us with any privacy request at the address below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
