🎨 Lalabuba — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Lalabuba ("we", "our", "the app") is a free AI coloring page generator for kids and families. This policy explains what information is collected when you use Lalabuba at lalabuba.com or via our iOS app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Lalabuba does not require you to create an account. We do not collect, store, or share:

  • Names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • The coloring pages you create or save
  • Your location
  • Payment information (the app is free)

2. Information That Is Collected

Usage analytics (Google Analytics): We use Google Analytics to understand how the app is used in aggregate — e.g. which features are popular and what languages users prefer. Google Analytics collects anonymised data such as browser type, device type, and pages visited. No personally identifiable information is sent. You can opt out via the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Bot protection (Cloudflare Turnstile): When you click "Draw!", an invisible Cloudflare Turnstile challenge runs to verify the request comes from a human. Turnstile processes your request without setting tracking cookies. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy for details.

AI image generation: The subject text you type (e.g. "dragon", "butterfly") is sent to our server and forwarded to an AI image generation service (HuggingFace) to create your coloring page. This text is not stored beyond the duration of the request.

Shared coloring pages: If you use the "Challenge a friend" feature, your coloring page image is temporarily stored (up to 7 days) on Vercel Blob storage to generate a shareable link. It is automatically deleted after 7 days.

3. Children's Privacy

Lalabuba is designed to be safe for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app contains no advertising, no in-app purchases, and no social networking features. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics — anonymised usage statistics
  • Cloudflare Turnstile — bot protection
  • HuggingFace — AI image generation
  • Vercel — hosting and temporary image storage

5. Cookies

Lalabuba uses minimal cookies. Google Analytics sets anonymised analytics cookies. Cloudflare Turnstile does not set tracking cookies. No marketing or advertising cookies are used.

6. Data Retention

Shared coloring page images are deleted automatically after 7 days. Analytics data is retained by Google for 26 months per their standard policy. No other data is stored by us.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is generally nothing to access, correct, or delete. For any privacy concerns, contact us at the address below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.

9. Contact

For privacy questions: privacy@lalabuba.com
