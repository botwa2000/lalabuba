Lalabuba turns any word into a color-by-number coloring page in seconds — and it's completely free, with no ads.

Type any subject — a dragon, a unicorn, a castle, a penguin — and Lalabuba instantly creates a custom coloring page just for your child. Then color it in by number, tap to fill, or free-draw with the pencil.

HOW IT WORKS
• Type anything you want to draw
• Tap a numbered region, then tap a color to fill it
• Free-draw and add your own touches with the pencil
• Print your masterpiece or save it to your gallery
• Challenge a friend — share the same picture and race to finish!

REWARDS THAT KEEP KIDS CREATING
• Earn 30+ collectible stickers across fun groups: Milestones, Day Streaks, Explorer, Creativity and Sharing
• Complete a new Daily Mission every day
• Unlock colorful crayon packs — Neon, Candy and Galaxy
• Decorate your very own penguin mascot with the stickers you earn

FEATURES
• AI-generated coloring pages — endless creativity
• Color by number with 6 to 24 colors, or pick Max colors
• 4 difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, Hard, Extreme
• Crayon, pastel, nature and unlockable color palettes
• Print or save your finished artwork
• Challenge mode: share and compete via QR code or link
• Available in 12 languages
• Works on phones and tablets
• 100% free — no account needed, no ads, no in-app purchases

PRIVACY FIRST, MADE FOR FAMILIES
No account, no login, no ads, and no personal data collected from your child. External links are behind a parental gate.

Perfect for kids ages 3–12 — and creative people of all ages!
