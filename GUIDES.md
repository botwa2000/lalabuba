## HOW APP STORE CONNECT IS STRUCTURED

When you open appstoreconnect.apple.com and click on Lalabuba, you land on
the app overview. The page has a LEFT SIDEBAR with several tabs:

  App Store          ← your listing, screenshots, description, keywords
  TestFlight         ← builds uploaded by Codemagic
  App Analytics      ← downloads, revenue
  App Information    ← categories, privacy policy URL, content rights
  Pricing and Availability  ← free/paid, countries
  Subscriptions
  In-App Purchases

Most of your work is split between "App Store" and "App Information".

- [x] Apple Developer Account active ($99/year)

- [x] App created in App Store Connect (Apple ID: 6761691648)

## STEP 1 - APP INFORMATION (categories, privacy policy)

NAVIGATION:
1. Go to appstoreconnect.apple.com
2. Click "My Apps" in the top navigation bar
3. Click on "Lalabuba" in the app list
4. In the LEFT SIDEBAR, click "App Information"

You are now on the App Information page. Scroll down - you will see several
editable sections. Find and fill in:

APP NAME (at the top of the page):
  > Click the text field next to "Name"
  > Value: Lalabuba
  (This is already set when you created the app - just verify it)

SUBTITLE:
  > Find the "Subtitle" field below App Name
  > Click it and type: AI Coloring Pages for Kids
  (This shows under the app name on the App Store listing - max 30 chars)

PRIMARY CATEGORY:
  > Find the "Primary Category" dropdown
  > Click it > scroll to "Education" > select it

SECONDARY CATEGORY:

**Free tier - no credit card required.**

Together AI gives free access to open-source AI models including the one the app uses.

5. Copy the key - it starts with `together-` - **you only see it once**
